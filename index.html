        Hybrid SPX 0DTE Strategy — Overview

        This is the hybrid SPX 0DTE strategy. It classifies days into three distinct types:

        Teal Days — Straddle

        • Traded between 9:32 AM and 1:30 PM.

        Purple Days — M8 Butterfly

        • Based on M8’s recommendation, with entry times varying by day of the week.
        • This setup is less effective without TTV software, which helps identify optimal entry windows for butterflies.
        • I’ll try my best to update the best entry times weekly on my webpage.

        Yellow Days — BvS Butterfly

        • Traded at 9:36 AM, centered around a major positive gamma value taken from 9:35 AM gex-bot-stream feed.

        Rules for Day Classification

        Here’s the logic:

        • Check SPX overnight gap at the open:
          • If the gap (up or down) is greater than 0.9%, it’s a Purple Day.
          • If the gap is less than 0.9%, check the next items:
        • Open-to-open VIX: Yesterday’s VIX open minus today’s VIX open.
          • If this value is more than 1.4, it’s a Purple Day.
          • If not, continue to the next condition.
        • Check overnight VIX drop: (Yesterday’s VIX close at 4:15 PM minus today’s VIX open at 9:30 AM)
          • If the drop is more than 0.65, it’s a Yellow Day.
          • If the drop is between 0.01 and 0.65, it’s a Teal Day.
          • If none of the above conditions are met, it’s a Purple Day.
        • If a Purple Day falls on a FED day, skip the trade.
        • On Teal Days, if the straddle doesn’t trigger by 1:30 PM due to high VIX, cancel the order — that’s expected.
        • On CPI days, we use only one filter: overnight VIX movement. If the VIX is up, we don’t trade; if it’s down, we trade straddles.
        • If the M8 SPX Butterfly prediction has a 0% win rate for the entire day, then the next day we automatically take a 9:32 AM Straddle trade, using the same rules as the overnight VIX-down (Teal day) straddle.
        • On non-Fed, non-CPI Wednesdays, if a straddle signal appears, replace it with M8 butterflies.

        Money Management

        • Butterflies (Purple & Yellow Days):
          Max risk is $1,400. Widen the wings until the debit is close to this number. If the next strike pushes the cost over $1,400, it’s acceptable to settle near $1,000.
        • Straddle (Teal Days):
          Max risk is $3,200, the same as used in Mad’s strategy.
        • All trades are held to expiration.
